A sound will play from the speaker at 0 degrees azimuth. 

- Set the SLM dial to 60, response to “Slow”, and weighting to “C”. 

- Enter the testing booth

- Place the SPL meter at the location of the listener’s head with the sensor facing the active speaker. 

- Meter should read 65 dB (±1 dB). If not, see Troubleshooting.
